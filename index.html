ASSA Research Collaboration Initiative

Connecting researchers and students for academic growth and innovation.

Overview Objective

  • Enhance ASSA's research involvement and members' academic growth.
  • Connect researchers with enthusiastic student collaborators.
  • Strengthen ASSA’s professional presence (LinkedIn focus).
  • Foster equal access to research opportunities.

Key Components

A. Platform Development

  • Identify IT support or access to current ASSA website management.
  • Enhance website or link a new page for researcher registration.
  • Details collected: Project title, description, supervisors, institution, remote options.
  • Alternative option: Google Forms.
  • Timeline: Estimated <1 month.

B. Promotion Strategy

  • Create new ASSA LinkedIn page.
  • Promote the platform and researcher sign-up forms on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook.
  • Spotlight posts for committee members and share success stories.

C. Research Matching & Outreach

  • Review and categorize submitted topics by field, location, accessibility.
  • Collect members' research interests and current commitments.
  • Outreach to universities, institutions, and researcher networks.

Member Involvement Requirements

  • Submit research profile and interests.
  • Provide current project commitments for fair matching.
  • Share previous research collaborator contacts if available.
  • Participate actively in assigned projects and create a LinkedIn profile.

Benefits

For ASSA Members:

  • Access to structured research opportunities.
  • Development of research, writing, and collaboration skills.
  • Increased chances of co-authorship and publications.
  • Professional exposure through ASSA promotions.

For Researchers:

  • Student collaborators to assist in projects.
  • Reduced workload and enhanced project output.
  • Opportunity to mentor future professionals.
  • Certificate of participation (TBC).

For ASSA’s Professional Image:

  • Showcases leadership and innovation.
  • Expands ASSA’s academic network and visibility.
  • Strong LinkedIn and professional presence.

  • Number of researcher sign-ups and student matches.
  • Growth in social media engagement.
  • Bi-weekly progress checks and a major 3-month review.
  • Potential project termination if insufficient progress at 3 months.
