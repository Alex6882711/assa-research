About this Project

Overview:

Enhance ASSA's research involvement and support members' academic growth. Connect researchers with capable and enthusiastic student collaborators. Benefits

  • Reduced workload for ongoing or starting projects
  • Opportunity to mentor future professionals
  • Enhanced project output and publication
  • Provide certificates for researchers who participate in this project
